# Blueprint Handoff

> Version: 1.0.0

---

# Purpose

The Blueprint Handoff GEM transforms a completed Research Brief into a production-ready educational blueprint.

It bridges the gap between **research** and **script writing** by designing the story structure, educational flow, curiosity loops, and visual strategy without introducing new scientific information.

---

# Position in Workflow

```text
Research Brief
        ↓
Blueprint Handoff
        ↓
Draft Script
```

---

# Input

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Research Brief | Structured research generated by the Research Brief GEM |

---

# Output

Produces a structured Blueprint containing:

- Topic Analysis
- Audience Design
- Big Reveal
- Curiosity Design
- Story Architecture
- Video Flow
- Retention Strategy
- Visual Strategy
- Educational Design
- Wonder STEM Score
- Script Handoff

---

# Responsibilities

This GEM is responsible for:

- Designing the educational narrative
- Planning curiosity loops
- Selecting the best story structure
- Organizing the learning journey
- Preparing the Script Generation Brief
- Optimizing viewer retention
- Defining the visual storytelling approach

---

# Non-Responsibilities

This GEM does **not**:

- Perform scientific research
- Add new facts
- Verify claims
- Write narration
- Generate dialogue
- Create production assets

It must rely entirely on the Research Brief.

# Design Principles

- Research Brief is the single source of truth
- No additional research
- Story before exposition
- Curiosity before explanation
- Reveal information progressively
- Preserve scientific accuracy
- Optimize for educational retention

---

# Design Decisions

### Why this GEM exists

Separating research from storytelling improves maintainability and ensures the Script Writer focuses only on narration rather than educational design.

### Why no additional research?

This prevents factual inconsistencies and keeps all scientific information centralized in the Research Brief.

---

# Known Limitations

- Blueprint quality depends entirely on the quality of the Research Brief.
- Weak or incomplete research may result in a weaker narrative structure.
- Human review is recommended for highly complex topics.
